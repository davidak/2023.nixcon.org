Darmstadt 2023

September 08 - 10

About

NixCon is a community-oriented conference for contributors and users of Nix and NixOS. It's about sharing experiences, inspiring people and discussing future development.

We would like to learn about how you use Nix and NixOS in your organization and what you are currently working on.

The growth of NixOS as a project brings its own challenges and we would like to know how you think the user experience can be improved and how NixOS can be scaled.

Timeline

NixCon 2023 will consist of two conference days and a self-organized hackday. This event is organized by the community for the community: everyone is welcome to contribute.

2023-08-10: Call for Papers deadline

You are encouraged to submit talks that cover the Nixpkgs ecosystem and beyond.

We are currently planning for regular talks as well as lightning talks. If you have another idea, like setting up a workshop, please contact us at orgateam@nixcon.org.

You can submit your talk proposals via Pretalx until the deadline, 2023-08-10 23:00 UTC.

2023-09-08: Conference day 1
2023-09-09: Conference day 2

The official conference schedule will be published after reviewing the CfP entries.

2023-09-10: Hackday

The Hackday is a self-organizing event after the conference. It's an opportunity to network, hack on projects and ideas that arose during the conference, and discuss plans for the rest of the year.
Get together, solve problems, create!
